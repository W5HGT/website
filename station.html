The Shack

We do most of our work out of a small closet in Nethken Hall. Our main radio is a Kenwood all-band transceiver, but we also have a an HF ICOM and a VHF Realistic radio.

Nethken Antennas

On Nethken's roof we have several antennas for working HF, VHF, and UHF. There are two HF dipoles, a fiberglass VHF antenna, and a rotor mount.

Wyly Tower Repeater

We have a 2-meter repeater based in the penthouse of Wyly Tower. It is an old GE Executive radio with a CAT-1000 Repeater Controller. It was built by long-time friend of the club, Kevin Thomas.
